Capabilities of WURFLM.JS

  • WURFL.js can detect the models of devices that are opening your website, a very handy tool when designing a responsive website.
  • One of the ways you can check the models is with some of the new functions introduced:
  • complete_device_name: Concatenation of brand name, model name and marketing name (where available) of a device into a single string. (Note that full iPhone model is only available in commercial WURFL.js Basic, Standard, or Pro licenses.)

    form_factor: May return any of these values: Desktop, Tablet, Smartphone, Feature Phone, Smart-TV, Robot, Other non-Mobile, Other Mobile

    is_mobile: Returns true if the device is wireless (e.g. Wi-Fi, 3G, LTE). Specifically, a tablet or mobile phone is considered wireless. Otherwise, a desktop or laptop are false.
